Serpenti Twist Watch
"Ready to twist your time again"

     Four new variations of the playful and unique BVLGARI Serpenti Twist watch enliven the new season. A dainty chain bracelet, with supple articulated links makes it unmistakable precious in the twinkling of an eye. The chain bracelet can be replaced by the coloured leather strap and vice-versa, thereby rendering the BVLGARI Serpenti Twist infinitely playful and irresistibly customisable.

  • OCTO WATCH "Break The Mold"

       Bvlgari claims its fifth world record with the new Octo Finissimo Chronograph GMT, the world’s slimmest self-winding watch endowed with chronograph and GMT function: an exciting blend of technical sophistication and aesthetic refinement.

  • Lvcea Watches 2018

       LVCEA’s inspired designer bracelet coils into a geometry of polished metals, surrounding the round watch face. The master of colored gemstones sets every LVCEA crown with a noble pink cabochon-cut stone, topped with a diamond. LVCEA watch brings glamour to the everyday and to every style.

  • Serpenti Seduttori Watch Collection

       Marking time with its signature gold design, the new Serpenti Seduttori casts a golden glow on all the beautiful moments in a woman’s life. With its unprecedented rose gold, yellow gold, white gold, and diamond pavé renditions, the collection writes a new chapter in the constant evolution of Serpenti. In line with Bvlgari’s jewellery-making heritage, the drop-shaped case is smoothly integrated with the scale-motif bracelet, celebrating the eternal renewal of an icon that was “born to be gold.”

  • LVCEA Mosaique Watch

       BVLGARI pays tribute to the ancient tradition of mosaics with the unique dials of the two new Lvcea watches, composed of tiles made of gold which creates a sparkling ballet of unique light effects. Mechanical movement with automatic winding. 33 mm, 18 kt rose gold case set with diamonds. 18 kt rose gold crown set with a pink stone and diamond. 18 kt rose gold mosaic dial. Galuchat bracelet. 18 kt rose gold ardillon buckle.
